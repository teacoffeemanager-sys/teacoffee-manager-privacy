Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 January 2025

TeaCoffee Manager ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles user data.

Information Collection and Use

TeaCoffee Manager does not collect any personal information such as name, phone number, email address, or location.

All data entered in the app including:

  • Tea and coffee consumption entries
  • Vendor details
  • Rates and app settings

is stored only on the user's device.

Local Storage & Backup

The app stores data locally using device storage. Backup files are created only when the user manually initiates a backup. No data is uploaded to any server.

Internet Usage

TeaCoffee Manager works completely offline for its core features. Internet access is not required for normal usage.

Advertisements

Future versions of the app may display advertisements using Google AdMob. Google may collect non-personal device information according to its own privacy policy.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, only you have access to your information.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.

Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
